About Vidsyn AI

We are on a mission to transform how the world learns from video content by turning hours of footage into smart, structured, and actionable summaries.

The Problem

In an era of digital learning, video has become the primary medium for knowledge. However, watching hours of video lectures, webinars, tutorials, or podcasts is incredibly time-consuming. Students and professionals struggle with information overload, difficulty in locating specific timestamps, and the tedious process of manual note-taking.

Our Objective

Vidsyn AI aims to solve this by providing an intelligent, automated video synopsis generator. By pasting a simple YouTube URL, users can instantly extract highly accurate transcriptions, generate structured summaries (bullet points or deep dives), and chat interactively with the video's content to get instant answers with cited timestamps.

Product Highlights

Core Features & Workflow

Vidsyn AI leverages state-of-the-art speech-to-text models and large language models to process video files in seconds. The system transcribes audio, structures the text, extracts key concepts, and formats them beautifully for study.

  • Accurate Transcripts: Generates fully timestamped transcripts.
  • Custom summaries: Offers short, medium, and long summaries depending on user needs.
  • Interactive Chat Q&A: Ask anything and get answers mapped directly to the video.

Key Benefits

Save 80% of Your Time

Skip the fluff. Get straight to the core takeaways of any lecture or tutorial in a fraction of the time.

Enhanced Retention

Reviewing structured text summaries and key terms helps reinforce concepts much better than passive watching.

Study Anywhere, Offline

Architecture

Our Technology Stack

A robust and scalable setup designed to deliver fast, secure, and accurate video processing.

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, and modular JavaScript for a responsive, modern, and high-performance UI.

Backend

Python FastAPI (future integration) for high-speed asynchronous API requests and seamless data handling.

Database

MySQL (future integration) to securely store user credentials, summary history, and system settings.

AI Processing

Whisper API for high-fidelity speech-to-text and OpenAI API for semantic summarization and interactive Q&A.
